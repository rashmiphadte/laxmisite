Our commercial services include customized turnkey solutions right from design, engineering, installation and post-installation support for chillers, ducted split ACs, double skin air handling units, piping packaged ACs and variable refrigerant flow (VRF) systems among other units.

We undertake all types of repairs, whether on site or in workshop for HVAC&R equipments, spares procurement and supply for different products and models at reasonable charges and estimates for repairs and Product upgradations suggestion after thorough technical study.

1. DESIGN: Our core team consists of highly qualified engineers with vast experience in the field of central and packaged air conditioning solutions in industrial and marine sector to provide cost effective design, product selection and installation for malls, offices, stores, entertainment complexes, hospitals, restaurants, retail outlets and marine vessels.

2. INSTALLATION: We select the best equipment for your requirements as per the space availability and cost effectiveness for installation of air conditioning systems. We are skilled at replacing HVAC, Electrical, Plumbing and various other systems in a cost effective way for retrofit and upgradation.

3. PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE: With our skilled team of technicians and engineers, we offer preventive maintenance services and provide repair and upgrades for all types of cooling and refrigerant products ranging from air cooled and water cooled air conditioners to variable refrigerant flow (VRF) systems. Our services has lead to an extension of the lifespan of commercial air cooling systems and enhancing its efficiency.

4. AMC SERVICES: We are a leading AC maintenance contractor undertaking Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMCs) for regular upkeep and upgradation of commercial air conditioning and refrigeration systems. Our major clients are from pharmaceutical industry based in Goa for undertaking Non-Comprehensive AMCs

5. WORKSHOP REPAIRS: Our workshop located at the office premises can cater for repair of air conditioning units including overhaul of all type of compressors and blower units. Repairs of condensing units including leak test and pressure test and assembling of major parts is undertaken at the workshop.

6. FABRICATION: We undertake all types of major fabrication of steel, Stainless steel and Galvanized piping systems for chillers and packaged ACs at industrial areas. Our expert technicians are capable for arc welding, Gas welding and Brazing jobs for piping and structures in industrial and Marine sector. We also undertake ducting work for new installation and repairs for air conditioning ventilation system.

7. INSULATION: We carry out insulation work in industrial, Pharmaceutical and Marine sector for Steam, Hot water and Chill water systems including ducting and ventilation systems.
